About Marcus

A trusted hand in the Valley of the Sun.

I'm Marcus Reeves, founder of Sun Valley Reverse here in Phoenix. For the past twenty-two years I've focused on one thing — helping homeowners 62 and older turn the equity in their desert home into a stronger, more comfortable retirement.

Reverse mortgages aren't a product to push; they're a tool to fit. My role is to listen first, run the numbers honestly, and make sure the math actually works for your life — whether that's eliminating a monthly payment, funding healthcare, or simply giving you breathing room to enjoy Arizona the way you've earned to.

Underwritten by Guild Mortgage, an Arizona-headquartered lender, every loan I close carries the backing of one of the Southwest's most established mortgage banks — paired with the kind of attention you can only get from someone who lives here.

Our City

Phoenix — where retirement finds its sun.

From the foothills of Camelback Mountain to the resort streets of the Biltmore, and along the quiet edge where Phoenix meets Scottsdale — the Valley of the Sun has become the country's most sought-after retirement landscape.

Every winter, thousands relocate here from Minnesota, Michigan, Illinois, the Northeast — drawn by the desert light, the dry air, and a slower pace of life. Many arrive with substantial equity in their longtime homes. A reverse mortgage often becomes the bridge that lets them settle into Arizona on their own terms.

I've spent my career in this market. I know what an Arcadia ranch trades for, how to value a Moon Valley patio home, and what a North Scottsdale appraiser will actually accept. That local fluency makes the difference between a loan that closes and a loan that gets stuck.
