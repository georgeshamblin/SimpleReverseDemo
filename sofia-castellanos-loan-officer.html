About Sofia

Tu socia de confianza in Miami reverse lending

Born in Havana, raised in Hialeah, and serving the Miami community for over twenty-one years, Sofia Castellanos has built her practice around one guiding principle: the reverse mortgage conversation should happen at the kitchen table, in the language the family speaks at home.

As Senior Reverse Mortgage Consultant at Costa Sol Reverse Mortgage, Sofia has guided more than 2,180 South Florida families through HECM, Jumbo, and purchase reverse mortgage solutions — funding over $610 million across Miami-Dade and Monroe counties.

She specializes in multigenerational households — particularly the Latin American extended-family structures common across Coral Gables and Westchester — and works closely with adult children, financial advisors, and elder-law attorneys to make every decision a family decision.

Our City

Miami: la ciudad where every generation belongs

Miami's senior population is the fastest-growing in Florida — fueled by retirees relocating from the Northeast, returning expatriates, and a deeply rooted local community whose families have called Miami-Dade home for forty years or more.

What makes our market distinct is the multigenerational household. Across Coral Gables, Westchester, Kendall and the Grove, three generations frequently share a single home. A reverse mortgage here is not just a financial product — it's a family planning decision, and it deserves to be discussed as one.

From Brickell condominiums to Coral Gables Mediterranean Revivals, Coconut Grove historic cottages to Key Biscayne oceanfront, Sofia structures reverse mortgage solutions tailored to South Florida's unique property mix, HOA dynamics, and insurance realities — including post-storm valuation considerations.
